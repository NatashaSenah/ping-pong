About Ping Pong

Table tennis, also known as ping-pong, is a sport in which two or four players hit a lightweight ball back and forth across a table using small bats. The game takes place on a hard table divided by a net.

Rules:

Ping Pong will count to your number from 1 with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 become'ping'
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 become'pong'
  3. Number divisible by 15 become'pingpong'
